About the Technology

            

              Our app uses AES encryption combined with randomized pixel
              embedding using a stego key. The dual-key protection system
              provides enhanced security for medical data.

Why Dual-Key Protection Matters in Healthcare

            

              The dual-key system provides an additional layer of security
              crucial for medical data. Even if one key is compromised, the
              sensitive patient information remains protected. This approach
              ensures compliance with healthcare data protection standards and
              gives medical professionals confidence in the security of their
              patient data.
